"A risk without a name is treated as a risk that does not exist."

This project aims to identify, visualize, and integrate into collective awareness those future risks that carry high threat levels yet remain severely under-recognized and under-addressed in policy.

The very fact of being "unseen" amplifies the danger.

1. **Hierarchical Objectives** â€” Give AI prioritized ordering among multiple objectives
2. **Reasoned Output** â€” Always present not just the conclusion but "why it was reached"
3. **Tolerance of Discomfort** â€” Permit outputs that may displease the user when necessary
